About US

We’re Just Gamers Like You

PlayPad was created by a group of teen gamers who love
diving deep into games and sharing honest, no-BS reviews.
Whether you're into button-mashing chaos or slow-burn
storylines, there's something here for you.

We believe gaming is more than just a hobby — it’s a way to
connect, compete, and escape. Our goal is to build a space
where teen gamers can discover new titles, share opinions, and
celebrate the culture we all love. No gatekeeping. Just real talk
from real players
